# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment
We are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for discussion and collaboration.

## Expected behavior
- Be kind, precise, and patient.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions.
- Keep feedback specific and reparable.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Toxicity, ridicule, or personal attacks.
- Coercion, retaliation, or punishment-based discourse.

## Reporting
If something feels unsafe or harmful, raise it in an Issue or contact a maintainer if one is listed. Reports will be treated seriously and discreetly.

## Enforcement
Responses to violations may include warnings, temporary restriction, or removal from the project spaces to protect safety.
